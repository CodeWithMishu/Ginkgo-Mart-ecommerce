About Ginkgo Mart

Your trusted partner for healthy, organic millet products since 2020

Our Story

Ginkgo Mart was born out of a passion for promoting healthy eating habits through the revival of traditional millets. Founded in 2020, we started with a simple mission: to make nutritious millet-based products accessible to everyone.

What began as a small family venture has now grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of health-conscious customers across India. We work directly with local farmers who practice sustainable agriculture, ensuring that every product that reaches you is of the highest quality.

Our journey is driven by the belief that food should not only nourish the body but also support our planet and the communities that grow our ingredients.

Our Mission

To promote health and wellness by providing premium quality millet-based products while supporting sustainable farming practices and empowering local farming communities.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved and trusted brand for healthy, organic food products, inspiring millions to embrace nutritious eating habits for a healthier tomorrow.
